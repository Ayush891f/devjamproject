IIT HYDERABAD

Placement reviews

The college provides 100% placements. The average salary package offered is 20 LPA. Companies like Google, Facebook, TCS, and many other reputed companies visited the campus for placements. The highest salary package offered is more than 1 crore per annum. The college provides both domestic and international placements. Internship data is not clear. There is a coding culture in college. If you like coding, then you will not find a better place than here in India.

infrastructure

The hostels have twin sharing rooms (till 3rd year), with common bathrooms, common laundry and monthly room cleaning services. There are 4 messes, north, south, non-veg and Jain mess to suit everybody. There are medical facilities available within the campus with an on-campus nurse and counsellor. Wi-Fi is available only in specific places in the campus and LAN is available in the hostel only after the first year of college. There is a library with all the required course textbooks

mess food reviews

Food in IIITH is one of the best food among all the colleges in the country. We have 4 messes. You can eat anyone of them (no restrictions). Food in canteens is also good. We have 3 working canteens.So, in the college food will never be a problem for anyone whether you are from North or South India.

academic reviews

Faculty members in our college are helpful to students in making them stronger and clever, and they never discourage us. They are well-educated, and almost half of the faculty members are from IITs of North India. They are knowledgeable, so they can easily make us brighter with their thoughts.The course curriculum is very well-designed by the institute. It is a research-based college. So, no industry exposure is provided, but you are a research student, you can have many advantages
