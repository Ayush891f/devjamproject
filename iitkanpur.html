IIT KANPUR

Placement reviews

lacement is good. 70% students are placed every year in good companies with a good intial stipend. College give special classes to prepare the students for placement and interviews. Students has no need to go for a coaching and to worry about placement.

infrastructure

nfrastructure is nice. The campus has very big. It has two floor library with access of e- resources. It has both boys and girls hostels and both are well maintained. The food provided in the mess is clean and tasty

mess food reviews

By food at IIT Kanpur, I assume you are referring to the Mess Food. Quite frankly, I thought the food was not good enough until I visited two other IITs Bombay and Madras. I visited these other IITs in quick succession and realised that the food in Kanpur is much better for most days of the week than Bombay and Madras. No offences to the food in these IITs and to the people who like the food there. Also, I would add that it is ultimately your own preference which matters because I have seen people who criticize the mess food like anything and also those who praise it like anything. As far as IIT Kanpur is concerned, you will get food almost 24 hours a day except very early in the morning so there are numerous options to choose from before you have a meal at any time of the day

academic reviews

Professors are well experienced and very cooperative. They have unique style of teaching which impressed the most. Students can easily pass out sem exam if he remains attentive in all the lectures. Curriculum is designed according to the industrial needs.
